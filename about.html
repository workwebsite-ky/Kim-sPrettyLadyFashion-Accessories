Welcome

Style that starts with you

Kim's Pretty Lady Fashion & Accessories was born from a love of fashion and an even bigger love for the women who wear it. Right here in Louisville, Mississippi, we set out to create a boutique where every woman feels welcome, celebrated and beautiful — no matter the size, the budget or the occasion.

We hand-select dresses, tops, jewelry, handbags and accessories that mix everyday wearability with that special spark. And because shopping should feel good, we're always here with honest, friendly styling help — never pushy, always in your corner.
